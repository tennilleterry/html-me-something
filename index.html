READING IS THE BEST HOBBY

My hobby is reading. I read story books, magazines, newspapers and any kind of material that I find interesting.

This hobby got started when I was a little girl. I liked reading novels, story books.I wasn't the type of kid to go outside and play, Id rather get lost in another world. I would often use people I knew or my favorite movie stars as the characters in the book. Sometimes I imagined myself as the characters. I can create a movie in my head.

  • Reading explores your horizons

    1. It gives you something new and different to enjoy

    2. There are many diverse options

    3. You can learn many different subjects

  • Reading stretches you mind

    1. It makes your mind be more creative than just watching a movie

    2. It gives your mind ability to interpret images in different way

    3. It keeps your brain active and happy

  • Reading increases your intelligence

  • Most importantly, you can time travel

    1. You can go to any period in time

    2. Ever wanted to know what it was like in the 1950s, just pick up a book

  • Mystery - Also known as, detective fiction, is a subgenre of crime fiction and mystery fiction in which an investigator or a detective either professional, amateur or retired investigates a crime, often murder.

  • Thriller - Thriller is a broad genre of literature, film and television, having numerous, often overlapping subgenres. Thrillers are characterized and defined by the moods they elicit, giving viewers heightened feelings of suspense, excitement, surprise, anticipation and anxiety.

  • Fantasy - Fantasy literature is set in an imaginary universe, often but not always without any locations, events, or people from the real world. Magic, the supernatural and magical creatures are common in many of these imaginary worlds.
